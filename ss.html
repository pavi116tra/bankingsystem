SAFE DIGITAL BANKING PRACTICES

Never share your account details such as account number, login ID, password, PIN, UPI-PIN, OTP, ATM / Debit card / credit card details with anyone, not even with bank officials.

Do not respond to messages asking for KYC updates or threatening account blocks.

Never install unknown apps that may access your personal data secretly.

Receiving money never requires scanning QR codes or entering MPIN.

Always use official websites and verify contact numbers.

Look for spelling errors in URLs. Use sites starting with "https".

Report unknown OTPs or debit alerts immediately to your bank.

Never reuse email passwords for banking or financial services.

Ignore fake offers claiming foreign remittance or lottery winnings.
